### PROJECT: eModal Visibility Platform - Terminal Operations & Logistics

**Duration:** Aug 2021 – Present

**Role:** Product Engineering Lead

**Synopsis:**

eModal is the technology fabric that connects Motor Carriers, Shippers and Logistic Service Providers with Terminal Operators & Port Authorities. Working from inside the terminal out, Terminal Operators configure eModal to ensure that truck missions are coordinated with servicing capacity by yard area, move and equipment type. All stakeholders enjoy advance visibility into yard & dispatch planning, vessel scheduling, cargo availability, container holds and fees due, as well as empty return instructions.
